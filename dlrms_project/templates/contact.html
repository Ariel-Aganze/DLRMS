Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for land registration?

Land registration requires proof of ownership, identity documents, land survey details, and payment of applicable fees. For a complete list of requirements, please visit our Services page or contact our office.

How long does the land registration process take?

The standard processing time is 14-21 business days after submission of a complete application. Expedited services are available for urgent requests.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money payments (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and in-person cash payments at our offices.

How can I verify the authenticity of a land certificate?

You can verify certificates through our Certificate Verification page by entering the certificate number and security code.
