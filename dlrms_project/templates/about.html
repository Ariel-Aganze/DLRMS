About DLRMS

Our Mission

The Digital Land Registration Management System (DLRMS) modernizes land administration in North Kivu, DRC through secure, transparent, and efficient digital solutions. We streamline land registration processes, reduce disputes, and ensure land tenure security for all citizens.

Our platform brings together landowners, government officials, surveyors, and legal professionals in a unified digital ecosystem that promotes accountability, reduces fraud, and accelerates service delivery.

Key Features

Enhanced Security

Advanced encryption and authentication protocols protect sensitive land information and personal data.

Streamlined Processes

Reduce registration time from months to days with automated workflows and digital submissions.

Transparency

Clear tracking of all land transactions with immutable record-keeping to reduce fraud and corruption.

Accessibility

Access services remotely from any device, reducing the need for in-person visits and paperwork.

Our History

1

Project Inception 2021

DLRMS was conceived as a collaborative initiative between the North Kivu provincial government and international development partners to address challenges in land administration.

2

System Development 2022-2023

Our team developed the DLRMS platform, focusing on security, usability, and integration with existing land administration frameworks.

3

Launch and Expansion 2024-Present

DLRMS launched with pilot implementations in key municipalities and is now expanding across North Kivu province with plans for nationwide adoption.
