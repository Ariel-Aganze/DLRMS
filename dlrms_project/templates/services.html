Our Services

The Digital Land Registration Management System provides a comprehensive suite of services for landowners, government officials, and other stakeholders in North Kivu, DRC.

Land Registration

Complete digital land registration process for new properties and updates to existing records.

  • • Online application submission
  • • Document verification
  • • Secure digital certificate issuance

Land Transfers

Streamlined processes for transferring land ownership between parties.

  • • Digital transfer applications
  • • Multi-party authentication
  • • Secure payment processing

GIS Mapping

Interactive geographical information system for land management and visualization.

  • • Satellite imagery integration
  • • Boundary verification
  • • Land use analysis

Dispute Resolution

Structured processes for resolving land disputes efficiently and fairly.

  • • Online dispute filing
  • • Evidence management
  • • Mediation scheduling

Digital Certificates

Secure issuance and verification of digital land ownership certificates.

  • • QR code verification
  • • Digital signatures
  • • Tamper-proof documents

Records Management

Comprehensive digital storage and retrieval of land records and documentation.

  • • Historical record access
  • • Secure document storage
  • • Advanced search capabilities

Our Process

1

Application

Submit your application through our online portal with required documentation.

2

Verification

Our team verifies your documents and land information for accuracy.

3

Processing

Applications are processed through our digital workflow system with regular status updates.

4

Certificate Issuance

Receive your secure digital certificate with unique verification features.
